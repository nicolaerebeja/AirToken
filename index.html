Stellar Blockchain

There are many factors that make Stellar Blockchain and ARCP a perfect fit.

Stable Coin

Stellar’s stable coin is key to providing financial services to the crypto ecosystem. It accomplishes this by issuing new and pegged stablecoins to an algorithm-based supply. Users can then use these coins for online payment, lending, borrowing, and stock market trading. Stellar’s stablecoins UST (USD) and EUT (EUR) will become the backbone of ARCP platforms FIAT to DeFi gateway.

Bridge for other blockchains

Stellar blockchain is the bridge for other smart-contract-based blockchains where UST is de facto decentralized currency. Specifically, UST constitutes the liquidity and utility across those blockchains. As a result, inter-blockchain collaboration becomes much easier, and ultimately it benefits the end-users.

Stellar’s vision matches the ARCP way

"You don’t have to be crypto-savvy to use crypto. Bring the power of DeFi applications to the hand of the consumer"- is in line with Bitlocus’ vision. Stellar and ARCP want to make crypto widely accessible for the average investor. With ARCP, we have simplified the crypto investment procedure to only a few basic steps.

Community

Stellar is the 3rd largest smart-contract platform in the world by total value locked (TVL). Consequently, Stellar’s community is already robust and always demands new projects. Powered by Stellar Blockchain, the ARCP ecosystem will rapidly grow thanks to heightened communication and expanded transactions.
